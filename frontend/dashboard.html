Executive Summary

SmogNet is a localized, AI-driven air quality intelligence platform designed specifically for Pakistan's unique atmospheric challenges. By integrating hyper-local environmental factors—such as brick kiln emissions, seasonal crop burning, and urban traffic density—we provide actionable, real-time insights that generic global models miss.

Locally contextualized alerts and health insights.
Health impact translation (e.g., cigarette equivalents).
Predictive scenario modeling for proactive policy making.
